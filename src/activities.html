Activities at Pacific Trails Resort

Hiking

Pacific Trail Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayak are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided birfwatching trips at sunrise several times a week.
