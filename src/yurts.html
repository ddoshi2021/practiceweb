The Yurts at Pacific Trails Resort

What is a yurt?
Our Luxury yurts are permenant structures four feer off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-sized bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxury camping experience also includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Bring sense of adveneture and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to desss for changing weather with layers of clothing.
